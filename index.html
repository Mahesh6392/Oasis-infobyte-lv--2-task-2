1937 -

Biography

Ratan Tata, born on December 28, 1937, in Mumbai, India, is a renowned industrialist and former Chairman of the Tata Group. He transformed the conglomerate into a global powerhouse during his leadership from 1991 to 2012. His visionary leadership expanded the group's presence in industries like automotive, steel, and information technology. Tata's philanthropic efforts through the Tata Trusts have significantly impacted education, healthcare, and rural development in India. He received numerous accolades and awards, including the Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan. Ratan Tata's legacy as a business icon and philanthropist remains an inspiration worldwide.
